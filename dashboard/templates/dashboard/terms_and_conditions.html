  • When not to use the assessment

 

SCNND recommends you to carefully read the instructions for use before you use the Capture.

 

Do NOT use the app to take the photo of a skin spot that:

  • Is close in color with the surrounding skin, for example a skin spot on (very) dark skin type (type V and VI per the Fitzpatrick scale) or sunburnt skin,
  • Has been previously damaged,
  • Is surrounded by non-intact skin (e.g. open sores, ulcers, bleeding, scabs),
  • Is under the nails,
  • Is close to a (visible) area with scar(s),
  • Contains foreign matter (e.g. marker, tattoo, sunscreen, skin cream, powder, etc.),
  • Is covered by a significant amount of hair,
  • Is on mucosal surfaces (e.g. lips, genitals),
  • Is on or near a skinfold or,
  • Is not on human skin.
